# Dialogue Asset Sources

All dialogue for Act 1 NPCs is paraphrased from canonical Diablo II in-game voicelines and quest text. Where exact wording was uncertain, D2-flavored paraphrasing was used to preserve tone and lore consistency.

## NPCs
- **Akara**: High Priestess of the Sisterhood, offers healing/ID services, quest-giver for Den of Evil & Search for Cain.
- **Charsi**: Camp blacksmith, offers repair/trade/imbue, quest-giver for Tools of the Trade.
- **Gheed**: Merchant/gambler, offers gamble/hire/trade services, no quest involvement.
- **Kashya**: Rogue captain, offers Rogue merc hire, quest-giver for Sisters' Burial Grounds.
- **Warriv**: Caravan leader, offers travel to Act II post-Andariel, references Sisters to the Slaughter quest.

All prose is embedded directly in JSON per project conventions (matching quests pattern). Optional `textKey` fields are included in schema for future i18n migration.

## Source
Paraphrased from Diablo II (Blizzard Entertainment, 2000) and Diablo II: Resurrected (2021). This is a private, non-commercial project. No monetization. See `.github/copilot-instructions.md` asset policy.
